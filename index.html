a smol & gentle art+tech cafe

we are here to be excited, encouraging, & creative near each other. we like comics and narratives and games and experimental technology, puppers & cats, tea & coffee. we live anywhere. currently we are hanging out in the cafe and socially doing inktober, but feel free to come by and spin up a topic, there's not really any rules except to be kind <3

to grow the most gentle environment possible, polychromatica is currently invite-only. ask a current member for an invite, or gather one or more friends who will join together with you and send an email to art@daiyi.co with a lil about yourselves (:
